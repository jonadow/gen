Generative Work

I have been working in generative and digital art for the past few years. It has allowed me to explore some of my favorite fields, mathmatics,algorithms software and art. The background movies are samples of some of the work I created in Processing, a java IDE which allows me to make video content as well as stills. The stills are made as either single frame works or as frames selected from video content. To see the stills click on the gallery button. The videos will cycle slowly through the video content or you can flip through them using the cycle button. The videos and gallery my take a few seconds to load.
