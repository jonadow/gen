Chris Dow

Generative Work

Chris Dow graduated from Carleton University in 1993 with a bachelor's degree in engineering and went on to study at the Ontario College of Art where he spent 1995/96 in Florence and graduated in 1997. Chris has worked as an engineer for the past 15 years in Japan, Australia and the United States while keeping active as a painter. Chris has shown his work in Florence, Vancouver and Toronto, as well as having been an active artist at loop for 6 years. Chris has work in private collections as well as with the Art Gallery of Ontario. This body of work is a new direction for him and he is developing work on a few different platforms using a variety of software environments.
